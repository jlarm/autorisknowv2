## SEO AI

Content Generation & Optimization

1. Auto-generate Meta Descriptions
   - Analyze post content and generate compelling, keyword-rich meta descriptions
   - Provide multiple variations to choose from
   - Ensure they stay within the 150-160 character sweet spot
2. SEO Title Suggestions
   - Generate optimized title tags that balance SEO and click-worthiness
   - Include relevant keywords while maintaining natural language
   - Check against character limits
3. Content SEO Analysis
   - Review content for readability, keyword usage, and structure
   - Provide an SEO score with specific improvement suggestions
   - Check for proper heading hierarchy (H1-H6)

Technical SEO Enhancements

4. Schema Markup Generation
   - Auto-generate JSON-LD schema based on content type (Article, BlogPosting, etc.)
   - Include author, publish date, images, etc.
   - Generate FAQ schema from content
5. Image Alt Text Generation
   - Analyze images and generate descriptive, SEO-friendly alt text
   - Consider context from surrounding content
6. URL/Slug Optimization
   - Suggest SEO-friendly slugs based on content
   - Ensure they're concise and keyword-rich

Content Enhancement

7. Keyword Research Assistant
   - Analyze content and suggest relevant primary/secondary keywords
   - Identify semantic keywords and LSI terms
   - Check keyword density
8. Content Gap Analysis
   - Compare against competitor content or best practices
   - Suggest missing topics or sections to add
9. Internal Linking Suggestions
   - Analyze content and suggest relevant internal pages to link to
   - Generate natural anchor text

User Experience Features

10. SEO Dashboard/Audit
    - Real-time SEO score in your post editor
    - Visual indicators for missing elements (meta, alt text, etc.)
    - Livewire component with live updates as users type
11. Bulk SEO Optimization
    - Queue job to analyze and suggest improvements for existing posts
    - Generate missing meta descriptions for old content
12. A/B Testing Suggestions
    - Generate multiple variations of titles/descriptions for testing
